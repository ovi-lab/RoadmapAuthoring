 A Gameobject/Transform that serves as a parent for grouping Placeable Objects. This object will ask for and align itself to Geospatial Anchors, and keep record of any child Placeable Objects. During the session it is first created, its world position is flexible; it will align itself to a new Geospatial Anchor any time tracking accuracy increases. By grouping Placeables under a single parent anchor, we can maintain their positions relative to each other better than if each Placeable was subject to individual Geo anchor accuracy. More...
